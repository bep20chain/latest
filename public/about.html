Frequently Asked Questions

The verification process typically takes 1-2 minutes to complete. However, during times of high blockchain network congestion, it may take slightly longer to retrieve the necessary data.

If verification fails, you'll receive detailed information about why it failed. Common reasons include insufficient USDT balance, lack of BNB for fees, or missing token approvals. You can address these issues and try the verification again.

Once your transaction is successfully verified, our team will generate a secure certificate with a unique ID. This certificate will be available for download and can also be accessed via a secure verification link that you can share with trading partners.

Currently, we support USDT on Binance Smart Chain (BSC). We plan to add support for Ethereum, Tron, and other networks in the future. Make sure your wallet address is on the BSC network when submitting for verification.
